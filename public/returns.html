Returns & Refunds Policy

Your satisfaction is our priority

Our Return Policy

At AleppoGift, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you're not happy with your order, we offer a hassle-free 30-day return policy.

Important: Items must be unused, in their original condition, and in the original packaging with all tags attached.

Eligible Items

  • Unopened products in original packaging
  • Items with all original tags and labels
  • Products that are not personalized or custom-made
  • Items purchased within the last 30 days

Non-Returnable Items

  • Personalized or customized products
  • Items marked as "Final Sale"
  • Gift cards
  • Perishable goods
  • Opened or used items
  • Items without original packaging or tags

How to Return an Item

Contact Us

Email us at returns@aleppogift.com or call +971-56-112-5320 within 30 days of receiving your order. Please include your order number and reason for return.

Receive Return Authorization

Our customer service team will review your request and provide you with a Return Authorization (RA) number and return shipping instructions.

Package Your Item

Securely pack the item in its original packaging, including all accessories, manuals, and tags. Include your RA number on the package.

Ship the Item

Ship the package to the address provided. We recommend using a trackable shipping service. Keep your tracking number for reference.

Receive Your Refund

Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll process your refund within 5-7 business days.

Refund Information

Refund Processing

After your return is received and inspected, we will send you an email notification. If approved, your refund will be processed and automatically applied to your original payment method.

Processing Time: Please allow 5-7 business days for the refund to appear in your account. Depending on your bank or card issuer, it may take additional time.

Partial Refunds

In certain situations, only partial refunds may be granted:

  • Items with obvious signs of use
  • Items not in their original condition or missing parts not due to our error
  • Items returned more than 30 days after delivery

Shipping Costs

  • Standard Returns: You are responsible for return shipping costs
  • Defective/Incorrect Items: We will cover return shipping costs
  • Original shipping charges are non-refundable
  • If you received free shipping, the actual shipping cost will be deducted from your refund

Exchanges

We currently do not offer direct exchanges. If you need a different size, color, or item, please return the original item and place a new order for the desired product.

Tip: To ensure the item you want is available, consider placing your new order before returning the original item.

Damaged or Defective Items

We carefully inspect all products before shipping. However, if you receive a damaged or defective item:

  1. Contact us immediately at info@aleppogift.com
  2. Provide photos of the damaged item and packaging
  3. Include your order number

We will arrange for a replacement or full refund, including return shipping costs.

International Returns

International customers are responsible for return shipping costs and any customs fees. We recommend using a trackable shipping service for international returns.

Note: Customs duties and taxes are non-refundable.
